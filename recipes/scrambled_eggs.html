Soft, fluffy scrambled eggs are a simple yet tasty dish that can be eaten on their own or with toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until smooth.
  2. Heat the pan: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the eggs in and stir gently with a spatula until they are soft and just set.
  4. Season & Serve: Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then enjoy warm!

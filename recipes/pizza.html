How to Make a Homemade Pizza üçï

Making pizza at home is easier than you think! With a few basic ingredients and simple steps, you can enjoy a delicious, homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 2.5 cups flour
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2.5 tsp yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Sauce:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
    • 0.5 tsp salt
    • 0.5 tsp black pepper
  • Toppings:
    • 1.5 cups mozzarella cheese
    • Any toppings you like (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Make the Dough
    • Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes.
    • Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead for 5 minutes.
    • Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare the Sauce
    • Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    • Preheat oven to 220¬∞C (425¬∞F).
    • Roll out dough and place on a baking tray.
    • Spread sauce, add cheese, and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake
    • Bake for 12 to 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
